# Edventure
Aiming to bridge the gap between potential and access, Edventure is an AI-powered opportunity matching platform. Edventure utilises CV analysis and interest matching to curate personalized recommendations of opportunities such as scholarships, competitions, internships and volunteer programs. We collect and structure data of hundreds of programs, both international and national, to be consulted by our intelligent filtering system.
